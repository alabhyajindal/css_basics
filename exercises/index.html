Bath is a historic city in Somerset, England, known for its Roman-built baths and Georgian architecture. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and attracts millions of tourists each year.

Historial Sites

  • Roman Baths - Ancient bathing site
  • Bath Abbey - Historic church
  • The Circus - Georgian architecture
  • Royal Crescent - Iconic crescent-shaped terrace
  • Pulteney Bridge - Picturesque bridge

Education

Bath has two universities:

  • University of Bath
  • Bath Spa University

Media

Bath's local newspaper is the Bath Chronicle, owned by Local World. Published since 1760, the Chronicle was a daily newspaper until mid-September 2007, when it became a weekly.
